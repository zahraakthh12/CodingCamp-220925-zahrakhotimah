About The Company

RevoU is the largest professional live learning platform in Indonesia, helping ambitious students and driven employees upskill to become the best professional versions of themselves.

Founded in 2020, RevoU has helped over 3,000 students and professionals to achieve their career goals through our comprehensive programs in Digital Marketing, Data Analytics, and Software Engineering.
